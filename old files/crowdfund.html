Why is Ice Trust crowdfunding?

Ice Trust's mission is to preserve nature. We do this by buying large plots of land and adding it to our nature reserve.

Ice Trust is non-profit and non-governmental. We rely on donations from individuals who share our goals and vision. In return, donors become part of the Ice Trust community and can take honourship of their very own plot in the nature reserve.

We want to build the community from the start and invite you to become a founding member of Ice Trust.
