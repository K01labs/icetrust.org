What is Ice Trust?

Ice Trust is a non-profit organisation with a single objective: Preserve Icelandic wilderness.

You can help by taking honourship The state of protecting something that should not be owned of a plot of unspoiled wilderness managed by Ice Trust.

What you get

Honourship: Take honourship The state of protecting something that should not be owned of your very own plot of land in Iceland*

Location: Select your own plot location

Access: Your plot is available for you to visit at any time

Verified: You get precise coordinates for your plot, which are kept in a public registry (unless you wish to remain anonymous)

Permanent: Your honourship is permanent. You can keep your plot and pass it down generations
