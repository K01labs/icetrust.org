Updates

2024-11-09

An update on where we are, and where we're heading.

Land acquisition is complex. Property evaluations, regulatory approvals, partner alignment, it moves slower than a crowdfunding campaign. We've been doing the work, but we haven't been telling you about it.

Your money is secure. All €9,000 remains untouched in our dedicated green account. No operating costs. No salaries. Just your contribution, growing.

What's actually happening:

  • Active discussions with multiple potential landowners
  • Vetting properties against strict conservation criteria
  • Building partnerships that require patience and trust

Moving forward, you'll hear from us quarterly. Real progress or real challenges, whatever the quarter brings. We underestimated how much communication matters for a project like this. We're correcting that now.

2024-05-18

We are excited to share that we have identified new potential lead donors and are actively approaching them. Your support continues to drive us forward in our mission to protect Icelandic wilderness.

2023-07-29

Our website has been updated to better reflect our journey and progress. Financially, all crowdfunding contributions remain untouched and secure in a dedicated account. These funds have even earned interest, enabling us to protect even more nature in the future!

2022-09-01

Despite economic uncertainties, we remain steadfast in our commitment. We are continually exploring new avenues to ensure the sustainability of Ice Trust's mission.

2022-02-08

We are currently in discussions with larger donors to secure the necessary funds for the next big step for Ice Trust. Thank you for your patience and continued belief in our cause.

2021-10-15

All crowdfunding contributions have been received and are now safely transferred to a dedicated “green" interest account at one of Iceland's largest banks. This ensures that every euro contributes to our conservation efforts.

2021-08-07

We are thrilled to announce that our initial crowdfunding campaign was a resounding success! We raised €9,000, surpassing our target by 113%. This marks the beginning of a new and exciting chapter for Ice Trust.

2021-06-23

Our crowdfunding campaign is now live! We are excited to embark on this journey with your support to preserve Icelandic wilderness.
